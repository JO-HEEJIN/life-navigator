  • Data: NASA Satellites (SMAP, MODIS, Landsat)
  • Analysis: Soil health, vegetation, temperature
  • Output: Irrigation recommendations
  • Domain: Agriculture

Life Navigator

  • Data: Personal APIs (Gmail, Calendar, Fitness)
  • Analysis: Stress, schedule, health patterns
  • Output: Life optimization recommendations
  • Domain: Personal Productivity
